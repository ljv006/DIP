This program can equalize the histogram of a picture and perform different kinds of picture enhancement of a picture including smoothing, sharpening and high-boosting. 
You should first put the picture in the folder where you put the cpp file. 
You can enter "h" to equalize the histogram and you will get the source piture with its histogram and the result picture with its histogram. 
You can also enter "s" to start spatial filtering. There are three choices. You can enter "1" to 
smoothing and enter "2" to sharpen the picture and enter "3" to do the high-boosting. The pictures produced by the program will be stored in the folder you put the cpp file.
